Best Dragon Ball characters

Gohan

Gohan is the son of Goku and Chi Chi, making him a human/saiyan hybrid. He is the main character of the cell saga.

Future Trunks

Future Trunks is the son of Vegeta and Bulma, making him a human/saiyan hybrid. He is from a future where androids took over earth, and he went back in time to warn the Z fighters of said androids

Cell

Cell is an android created with the cells of the strongest fighters in the universe, including all the z fighters and even their enemies. Cell is the main villain of the cell saga as he attempted to defeat the z fighters and become the perfect being.
